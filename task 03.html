Fast & Furious

Fast & Furious is an action-packed movie franchise that revolves around high-speed car chases,
daring heists, and an unbreakable sense of family. Starting with underground street racing
in the first film, the series has evolved into a global saga featuring espionage, massive
stunts, and world-saving missions. Led by Dominic Toretto, played by Vin Diesel, and his
loyal crew, the films are known for their adrenaline-fueled sequences, stunning visuals,
and over-the-top action. Each installment adds new characters, higher stakes, and
more heart-pounding moments, making Fast & Furious a worldwide phenomenon loved
by action and car enthusiasts alike.
